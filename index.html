  1. How does Perceived Neighborhood Safety vary for individuals with different race/ethnicity?

Project Description: This cross-sectional study collected data on 1329 racially and ethnically diverse adults. a large proportion of the sample also identifies as Sexual Gender Minority (SGM). The goal of the original study is to identify whether adult SGM subgroups differ in terms of their mental and physical health behaviors and risk, as well as factors that affect the health of those with diverse sexual and gender identities.

Here, we use several data visualizations to explore the data before constructing strata for analyses using Multilevel Analysis of Individual Heterogeneity and Discriminatory Accuracy (MAIHDA).

Data Source: Health Behaviors of Sexually and Gender Diverse Individuals (PIs: Dr. Austin Folger and Dr. Nichole Kelly).
